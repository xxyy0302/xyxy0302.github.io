ABOUT US

Our REACH N’ JOY Café is located at the Setapak area - PV128. Our Café is a Simple But Charming White-Themed Café.

Our first goal was to give wonderful food and drinks to our customers. It is also our primary goal to provide a quiet and comfortable environment for our customers to rest or work in.

Our café provides a warm and inviting environment for all members of the family and friends to get together and have a good time.

Highest quality are used for every ingredients in our Café, to ensure our customers to have both treats for the mind and the palate. Every cup of beverage is made with love, by well-trained baristas. Desserts are all freshly prepared on a daily basis to ensure its’ delightful flavour can be enjoyed by tasters. Our Café always put our customers’ satisfaction first at all times.
